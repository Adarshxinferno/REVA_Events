Let's Go

Under 25 Summit 2019

It’s a huge youth festival in India aimed at sharing ideas and inspiring each other to make a difference in the world. Basically people attend to see what others are up to and show off what they’re doing, whether they’re working with technology, cooking, designing, travelling, creating art, etc. Networking is also a big part of the event. You go there to be inspired and find others who want to work with you on new projects. Sometimes you can even make the right connection to score a job.
